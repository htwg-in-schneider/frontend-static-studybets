How It Works

Step 1 - Create your account

Create your free StudyBets account using your university email address to verify that you're an active student.
You'll receive start Credits to begin your first bets instantly.

Step 2 – Join or Create a Challenge

Join an existing challenge from your university — or create your own and invite others.
Bet using virtual Credits. No real money involved.

Step 3 – Earn Credits & Climb the Leaderboard

Win predictions, rank higher, and flex on the leaderboard.
Top players get recognition across campus.

About

StudyBets turns learning and campus life into a game.

Only verified students can join, create challenges, and bet with virtual Credits — no real money involved.

Our mission is to make education competitive, social, and fun while rewarding knowledge and curiosity.
